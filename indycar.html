Overview:

Arrow McLaren SP is the latest chapter in the storied history of McLaren Racing in INDYCAR and North America.

The new team’s journey began in 2020 – 40 years after McLaren last competed full-time in IndyCar - with an exciting and promising first season of competition.

The Car - Vuse Arrow McLaren SP Chevrolet

This car is McLaren's output of what they hope can be their championship winning car. IndyCars can reach up to 380 kph, so it is even more crucial to ensure that the car is both safe and dynamic enough to elevate it from the rest of the field.

Drivers

Felix Rosenqvist is a 30 year old IndyCar driver from Sweden. Rosenqvist joined Arrow McLaren SP in 2020, and is looking forward to his 2022 season.... He has had a distinguished racing career across many different motorsporting events, such as GT Racing, Formula 3, Formula E, karting, and Formula Renault. He was injured in a major crash during the 2021 Detroit GP, so he was not able to finish the remainder of the season.

Pato O'Ward is a 22 year old IndyCar driver from Mexico. O'Ward joined Arrow McLaren SP in 2020 as well, and has been very successful within his McLaren career. In 2021, he was heavily involved in the championship, being a contender to take the championship title. He ultimately finished 3rd in the standings after some impressive results during the season. He hopes to continue his success into this next 2022 season.
