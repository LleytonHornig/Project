Project Proposal

About the Website

This website will be focused on McLaren Racing. This will give a rundown of McLaren's Formula 1 team, their Indy Car team, and their newly added Extreme E team. It will include the current driver lineups, the model car, and their recent results. It'll basically be a fan-page for McLaren Racing Fans!

The Objective

The main objective of this website will be to promote and inform fans of the rich history of McLaren and give some context to behind the scenes of McLaren. This is important as Formula 1 is becoming one of the most widely watched sports in the world due to the Netflix documentary Drive to Survive, and Indy Car has a massive following in the US as well. If people are curious about McLaren in the motorsports world, they can use this site to become more knowledgeable.

Target Audience

My target audience is primarily current McLaren racing fans and fans that are new to motorsports and want to support a team. On a larger scale, my target audience could also include all motorsport fans as McLaren is a top-recognized organization. With McLaren at the forefront of racing, both new and current fans will be able to enjoy this content while the rest of the motorsport community can become informed as well.

Page Contents

Besides the homepage, sitemap, and contact pages, I plan on creating 5 pages dedicated to information regarding McLaren including:

  1. McLaren Formula 1 Racing
  2. McLaren Indy Car
  3. McLaren Extreme E
  4. McLaren's Records/Achievements
  5. McLaren's History and Foundation
