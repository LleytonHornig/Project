Overview:

This is McLaren's first year in Extreme E, as this newer sport has been growing in popularity. This was an excellent opportunity for McLaren to expand its racing potential into the world of all-electric SUV off-road racing, while also joining the movement of being more sustainable.

The Car - MX

The MX is McLaren's answer to making an all electric off-road vehicle. In it's first season, this car will promote not only McLaren racing's brand name, but also help in a shift towards more sustainable racing.

Drivers

Tanner Foust is a 48 year old driver and car enthusiast from Los Angeles in the USA. He is well known as for being a television host on the popular TV show Top Gear. He is also an acclaimed rally car driver, known for his exceptional car control.

Emma Gilmour is a 42 year old driver from Dunedin, New Zealand. She is an experienced competitor in New Zealand's Rally Championships, which she has won and been runner-up three other times. She is now expanding her talents to Extreme E where she is McLaren's first female driver, an absolute honor for her.
