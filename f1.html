Overview:

McLaren's Formula 1 Team is still very well regarded by the world of motorsport. In the past, they have won 12 Driver's Championships, 8 Constructor's Championships, and over 180 race victories.

Legends such as Ayrton Senna, Alain Prost, and Mika Hakkinen, amongst others, that are some of the best who ever drove in F1.

The Car - MCL36

The MCL36 is the official race car for McLaren's 2022 season. After heavy revising in F1 regulations, thousands of hours of effort were put forth into the design and manufacturing of the car to fit the new criteria for the new era of F1.

Drivers

Daniel Ricciardo is a 32 year old driver from Perth, Australia. Daniel has been with McLaren since 2021, signing a 2-year contract to stay with the team. In 2021, he finished in P8 in the Drivers Championship with a total of 115 points.

Lando Norris is a 22 year old driver from Glastonbury, UK. Lando has been with McLaren since 2019, and plans to stay with the team until the end of 2026. In 2021, he finished P6 in the Drivers Championship with a total of 160 points.

Management

Zak Brown has been CEO of McLaren Racing since 2018. He is a former racer himself, giving him the necessary experience to know what needs to be done on the driver's side of things. He is currently tasked with bringing McLaren back to the top of the F1 Constructors Championship, in which they have a rich history of success.

Andreas Seidl joined McLaren's F1 team in 2019, becoming the team's principle. He is responsible for managing the team both on and off track, and he reports directly to Zak Brown. He comes with years of experience being a former team principle for Porsche and working at BMW.
