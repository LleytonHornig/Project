Welcome to McLaren Racing!

McLaren has a long history of success in motorsport racing. Founded in 1968 after Bruce McLaren, McLaren Racing Group has earned the right to be amongst the top list of the best racing brands. This site will give an overview of the current racing landscape for McLaren's three different racing groups.

Formula 1

Formula One is the highest class of international racing for open-wheel single-seater formula racing cars, being one of the most popular and influential sports across the globe.

IndyCar

The IndyCar Series, currently known as the NTT IndyCar Series under sponsorship, is the highest class of regional North American open-wheel single-seater formula racing cars in the United States.

Extreme E

Extreme E is an innovative all-electric off-road series. This motorsport for purpose aims to use racing to promote sustainability by racing in remote terrains to raise climate awareness as well as diversity, by insisting that all teams consist of a male and female driver.
